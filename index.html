About us

Innomatics Research Labs

A leading training institute, Innomatics Research Labs offers courses in areas such as NASSCOM Futureskills Prime Certified Data Science, Python, AI, Data Analytics, Full Stack Development, and Digital Marketing. Our practical training is designed meticulously to meet industry needs and provide valuable certification upon completion, empowering individuals to excel in their careers.

We aspire to establish India as a leading hub for Artificial Intelligence and analytics, providing transformative education and expert training across diverse sectors like Banking, Retail, and Healthcare.

Our mission is to empower individuals through hands-on learning in cutting-edge technologies, connecting them with industry experts to enhance decision-making and drive business innovation. With over 40 trainers from top companies, we focus on developing a benchmark in data science and transforming various sectors through its application.

We dream of inspiring a new generation of data-driven leaders who will shape the future of their industries, harnessing the power of data science to drive innovation and create meaningful impact.
